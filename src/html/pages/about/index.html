About Me

Hey! Name's Jake. I'm currently a Computer Science major at Oklahoma State, looking to graduate by May 2017. I've always been the type to take interest in things with flashing lights and bright colors, as long as I get to put it together myself. I began programming when I was 18, and I've been hooked ever since. Nowdays, you can find me working on front-end UI, game engine architecture, or playing around with a Raspberry Pi. My language of choice right now is probably TypeScript, however I'm not afraid to take the plunge into a C/C++ codebase.

Other interests of mine include watching football, drawing, hiking, and watching planes fly overhead. I like keeping myself active by participating in mud-runs or playing rugby, but really I just like doing anything where I get an excuse to run. Walking on the beach with my girlfriend is what I dream of doing everyday, and a good bowl of pasta will always put me in a great mood.
